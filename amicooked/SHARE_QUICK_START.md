# Quick Start: Using the Share Feature

## For Users

### How to Share Your Results

1. **Get Your Analysis**
   - Open the app
   - Enter text or upload a screenshot
   - Wait for your "cooked" percentage

2. **Tap Share**
   - On the results screen, find the "Share" button
   - It's next to the "Save Me" button at the bottom

3. **Wait for Image Generation**
   - A dialog will appear saying "Generating shareable image..."
   - This takes about 1 second

4. **Choose Where to Share**
   - Your device's share sheet will open
   - Select your preferred destination:
     - Instagram
     - Messages
     - WhatsApp
     - Twitter/X
     - Facebook
     - Copy to clipboard
     - Save to Photos
     - And more!

5. **Done!**
   - Your beautiful result card is shared
   - Includes your percentage, verdict, and highlights
   - Watermarked with app branding

## Privacy Note

- Images are only stored temporarily on your device
- Nothing is uploaded to our servers
- You control where the image goes
- Auto-deleted by your system over time

## Troubleshooting

**Share button does nothing**
- Make sure you've completed an analysis
- Try closing and reopening the results screen

**Image quality is poor**
- This shouldn't happen (we use 3x resolution)
- Try generating the image again
- Check your device storage isn't full

**Can't find the share button**
- It's on the results screen
- Located next to "Save Me" button
- Has a share icon (â†—ï¸)

**Share sheet doesn't open**
- Your device might not support native sharing
- Try restarting the app
- Update your OS to the latest version
